The history of Pohang has been long.Pohang Steel (now POSCO) was born as a strong will of Chairman Park Tae-joon and was a witness of South Korean football. In 1984, he became a professional football team. There are countless trophies that Pohang has won as well as its long history. It is the team that won the AFC Champions League three times, K League championship five times, FA Cup four times.

In addition, since 2003, Pohang has set up a youth soccer system to find and cultivate talented people in the region early.
